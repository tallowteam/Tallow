POWERFUL FEATURES

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Built from the ground up with security, privacy, and performance as core principles, not afterthoughts.

Quantum-Resistant Encryption

ML-KEM-768 (formerly Kyber) provides post-quantum security, protecting your files from both current and future threats. NIST-approved and battle-tested.

Zero Cloud Storage

Files transfer directly between your devices without touching any servers. What you share stays between sender and recipient, period.

Automatic Discovery

Devices on the same network are automatically detected using mDNS. No manual IP addresses, no configuration, no hassle.

Blazing Fast Speeds

P2P connections mean you're only limited by your network hardware. Saturate gigabit connections with ease using WebRTC data channels.

Privacy by Design

Automatic metadata stripping, no tracking pixels, no analytics. We don't know what you send, when you send it, or to whom.

Cross-Platform

Use Tallow on any device: macOS, Windows, Linux, iOS, Android, or just a web browser. Same experience everywhere.
