Platform Features

Built for the future

Every feature designed with security, speed, and simplicity in mind.

01

Quantum-Resistant Encryption

Future-proof your transfers with ML-KEM (Kyber) post-quantum cryptography. Protected against both classical and quantum computer attacks, certified by NIST.

02

True Peer-to-Peer Transfer

Direct device-to-device connections via WebRTC. Your files never touch our servers—only you and your recipient have access. Zero-knowledge architecture by design.

03

Instant Discovery

Find nearby devices automatically with mDNS/DNS-SD. No pairing codes, no setup. Just open and transfer.

04

Unlimited File Size

Transfer files of any size without arbitrary limits. Stream efficiently with chunked encoding and adaptive bitrate.

05

Cross-Platform

Works seamlessly across Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, Android, and web. One protocol, every device.

06

Privacy First

No tracking, no analytics, no cloud storage. Forward secrecy ensures past communications stay secure even if keys are compromised.

Security that runs itself

Every transfer is automatically protected by multiple layers of cryptographic security. No configuration required—military-grade protection is enabled by default.
