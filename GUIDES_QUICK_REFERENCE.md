## Feature Guide

### Navigation Elements

**Breadcrumbs** (Top of page)
```
Docs > Guides > [Guide Name]
```
- Click any breadcrumb to go back
- Shows your location in documentation hierarchy

**Table of Contents** (Under hero)
- Links to major sections in the guide
- Click to jump to relevant section
- Helps you find information quickly

**Anchor Links** (In TOC)
- Each section has an ID for direct linking
- URLs like `#e2e-encryption` work directly
- Share specific section links with others

**Previous/Next Navigation** (Bottom of guide)
- Navigate between guides in order
- Easy way to continue learning
- Links show guide titles

**Related Topics** (Bottom of page)
- Cross-links to related guides
- Discover additional resources
- Expand your knowledge

### Content Patterns

**Steps** (Numbered Instructions)
- Numbered gradient badges (1-6)
- Clear descriptions for each step
- Sub-bullets for additional details
- Follow in order for best results

**Comparison Cards** (Side-by-side Info)
- Feature cards in grid layout
- Hover effects for interactivity
- Great for seeing differences
- Color-coded indicators (yes/no/limited)

**Callout Boxes** (Important Information)
- **Blue (Info)**: General information
- **Yellow (Warning)**: Important cautions
- **Green (Success)**: Positive information
- **Red (Error)**: Problems and solutions

**FAQ Cards** (Questions & Answers)
- Click to expand full answers
- Hover to highlight
- Search-friendly
- Covers common questions

**Feature Grids** (Visual Feature Lists)
- Icons with descriptions
- Multiple columns on larger screens
- Sub-lists for details
- Organized by category

## Reading Paths

### For First-Time Users
1. Start with **Getting Started** guide
2. Choose your transfer method:
   - Same network? → **Local Network Transfer**
   - Different location? → **Internet Transfer**
3. Want to understand security? → **Security Guide**

### For Security-Conscious Users
1. Read **Security Guide** first
2. Then learn your transfer method
3. Reference **Best Practices** section

### For Advanced Users
1. Skip Getting Started
2. Go directly to your needed guide
3. Use specific sections via table of contents
