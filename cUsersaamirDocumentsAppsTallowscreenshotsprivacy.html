1. Introduction

Welcome to Tallow. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have control over your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

TL;DR: We collect minimal data, store everything locally on your device, use end-to-end encryption, and NEVER sell your data. You can export or delete all your data at any time.

2. What We Collect

Data We Collect

  • Device Information: Device name (user-defined or auto-generated), Device ID (randomly generated), platform type (for compatibility)
  • User Preferences: Theme, privacy settings, notification preferences, transfer settings
  • Transfer Metadata: File names, sizes, types, transfer timestamps, peer device names (NOT file contents)
  • Device Lists: Favorite devices, recently connected devices (references only)
  • Network Information (Temporary): Local IP addresses for peer-to-peer connections (not stored permanently)

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • NO real names, email addresses, or phone numbers (unless you voluntarily provide them)
  • NO social security numbers or government IDs
  • NO financial information or credit card numbers
  • NO biometric data or health information
  • NO precise geolocation data (beyond local network discovery)
  • NO browsing history or search history
  • NO tracking cookies or advertising IDs
  • NO analytics or telemetry data
  • NO file contents (files are NEVER stored, only transferred)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide the file transfer service:

  • Enable peer-to-peer file transfers between your devices
  • Save your preferences and settings
  • Maintain transfer history for your convenience
  • Identify devices on your local network

We do NOT use your data for:

  • Advertising or marketing
  • Selling to third parties
  • Analytics or tracking
  • Profiling or behavioral analysis

4. Data Storage & Security

Local Storage Only

All your data is stored locally on your device using:

  • localStorage: Settings and preferences
  • IndexedDB: Transfer history
  • NO server-side storage: We do not maintain servers with your data

Encryption

  • End-to-end encryption using ChaCha20-Poly1305 and AES-256-GCM
  • Post-quantum cryptography (Kyber-1024, Dilithium) for future-proof security
  • Perfect Forward Secrecy (PFS) ensures past communications cannot be decrypted
  • Zero-knowledge architecture: we cannot access your files or data

Privacy Features

  • Metadata stripping (EXIF, location data) enabled by default
  • IP leak protection (WebRTC leak prevention)
  • Onion routing support for traffic obfuscation
  • Guest mode (zero data persistence)

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or disclose your data to third parties.

  • NO advertising networks
  • NO analytics services
  • NO cloud storage providers
  • NO marketing partners
  • NO data brokers

Relay Servers

When direct peer-to-peer connections are not possible (due to NAT/firewall), we use relay servers for NAT traversal:

  • Relay servers cannot decrypt your files (end-to-end encrypted)
  • Relay servers do not log or store your data
  • Relay servers are used only for connection establishment

6. Data Retention

  • File contents: NEVER stored (zero retention). Files are transferred directly and immediately discarded after transfer.
  • Transfer metadata: Stored locally until you delete it (via "Clear History" or "Delete My Data")
  • Settings: Stored locally until you delete them
  • Guest mode: Zero retention (all data session-only, cleared on exit)

7. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

GDPR Rights (EU/EEA Users)

  • Right to Access (Article 15): Export all your data using the "Export My Data" button above
  • Right to Erasure (Article 17): Delete all your data using the "Delete My Data" button above
  • Right to Data Portability (Article 20): Export your data in machine-readable JSON format
  • Right to Object (Article 21): Disable any feature or use Guest mode
  • Right to Rectification (Article 16): Edit your settings and preferences at any time

CCPA Rights (California Users)

  • Right to Know (Section 1798.100): See "What We Collect" section above
  • Right to Delete (Section 1798.105): Use "Delete My Data" button above
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale (Section 1798.120): Not applicable - we do NOT sell your data
  • Right to Non-Discrimination (Section 1798.125): All features available regardless of privacy choices

8. Cookies & Tracking

We do NOT use cookies or tracking technologies.

  • NO analytics cookies
  • NO advertising cookies
  • NO social media pixels
  • NO fingerprinting

We use only localStorage and IndexedDB for essential functionality (storing your preferences and transfer history on your device).

9. International Data Transfers

Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers to servers. Peer-to-peer file transfers occur directly between your devices and are end-to-end encrypted.

10. Children's Privacy

Tallow does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (COPPA) or 16 (GDPR). Since we do not require accounts or collect personal identifiers, our service is safe for all ages when used under parental supervision.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Displaying a notice on the website
  • Requiring acknowledgment of changes before continuing to use the service

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@tallow.app
  • GitHub: github.com/tallow-app/tallow

For GDPR or CCPA requests, we will respond within the legally required timeframe (30 days for GDPR, 45 days for CCPA).
