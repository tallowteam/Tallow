- **Background**: White (#FFFFFF)
- **Foreground**: Dark Gray (#0A0A0A)
- **Primary**: Blue (#3B82F6)
- **Secondary**: Light Gray (#F3F4F6)
- **Border**: Light Gray (#E5E7EB)

### Dark Mode
- **Background**: Near Black (#0D0D0D)
- **Foreground**: White (#FAFAFA)
- **Primary**: Blue (#60A5FA)
- **Secondary**: Dark Gray (#1F2937)
- **Border**: Dark Gray (#374151)

- **Green**: Success, Beginner (#10B981)
- **Blue**: Info, Intermediate (#3B82F6)
- **Purple**: Advanced (#8B5CF6)
- **Orange**: Warning (#F59E0B)
- **Red**: Error, Destructive (#EF4444)

## Interactive States

- **Search Input**: Focus ring, primary color
